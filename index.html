Why We Got Started

Netwise Youth was created to help the youth learn how to stay safe online. In a world that's more connected than ever to the internet, safe cybersecurity practices are required, especially for young students, as it enables them to be safe online while making sure their devices aren't compromised.

About Us

Welcome to NetWise Youth! We are an initiative aiming to empower young students with the necessary skills they need to be able to stay safe online. We take the students on a journey to mastering the essentials of cybersecurity. Within our course, they will learn how to stay safe online, recognize and avoid scams, and protect their personal information. After students complete the course, we provide them with a certification after they succsesfully pass the test!
